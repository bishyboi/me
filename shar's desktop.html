faq

faq
what software do you use?

  • illustration: Clip Studio Paint EX
  • animation: Clip Studio Paint EX, Adobe Animate
  • 3D: blender 2.83 LTS
  • feel free to check the rest of the tools i use in the Work window!

are your commissions open?

  • for commission work, please contact me via my work email.

what's your setup?

  • i use an XPPen Artist Pro 16 (Gen 2)!
  • i also use a TourBox Elite as a supplementary art tool
  • i've recently been able to build a PC for myself ! nobody asks the specs sorry this wasnt a frequently asked question

where do you get your sound effects?

  • i get most (if not all) my sound effects from zapsplat.com!

can i use your work as my icon/dp/header?

  • yes, go ahead! :)
  • just please credit me if possible (and please don’t claim the work as your own!), and i’m A-OK with it hehe there's no need to message me ✌⭐

can i repost your work?

  • please don't repost my animations!
  • for other content, please ask permission first via dm/email!
  • though as a general rule, please don’t repost my work in pages where they’re already posted by me, i already have socials for these, please feel free to share them instead!

can i use your work in an edit?

  • i generally don’t mind, but please credit me properly!
  • and if you can, please ask permission first via dm/email

who is the frog on your profile pic?

  • froggert !!
  • he is the first felt plush i've ever made!! he is a frog with a daisy on his head :)

i sent you a message a BILLION YEARS AGO and you still haven’t replied :(

  • sorry if i don’t get to your message!! T_T
  • i have a really heavy backlog of messages on twitter and instagram, and they get pretty overwhelming for me!
  • i do read a lot of the messages, and i really appreciate those who express enjoying what i enjoy making, thank u so much !! i just can’t reply to everyone given the volume T_T i hope you can understand!
  • for urgent matters, work, and other invitations, please email me instead! thank you!! 💗

  • yea :)
